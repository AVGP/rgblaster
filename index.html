RGBlaster

A colourful space shooter

Mission

Clear this sector from all asteroids, but beware: The asteroids are divided into three different groups: Red, Green and Blue asteroids.

Each group can only be destroyed by a bullet of the same colour, so you need to make sure to load the right type of ammunition.

As this is deep space, you only have limited supplies.
You have enough energy to fire 5 bullets of each type, but you can collect the floating orbs to increase your capacity.

Good luck.

Controls

Launch

When you're ready hit launch.
